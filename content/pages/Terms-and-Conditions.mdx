      **Effective Date: October 15, 2025**


      Welcome to The Pan-African Data Project. This site shares curated
      historical data and visualizations about the people, organizations, and
      events that shaped Pan-Africanism between 1900 and 1960. Our goal is to
      make this research freely available for teaching, learning, and further
      scholarship.

      You are welcome to browse, search, and cite materials from the Pan-African
      Data Project for educational and non-commercial purposes. We ask that you
      use the materials responsibly and acknowledge the project and its
      contributors when doing so.
